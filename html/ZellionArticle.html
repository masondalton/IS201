Zellion's Broken Heart

Hate for the Night Brigade

Posted by Wit on Novemeber 11th, 2023

Ah, the tale of Zellion and his faithful Spren, Auxiliary. A duo once inseparable, like a perfectly matched pair of boots, now a lonesome warrior with a silent Shardblade. Auxiliary, a Spren of rare courage and loyalty, sacrificed itself in a moment as noble as any song sung by the minstrels of Kharbranth.

Friendship

Imagine, if you will, a bond as deep as the chasms of the Shattered Plains, a companionship that transcends mere words. Zellion and Auxiliary, together they faced challenges that would make even the bravest Alethi blanch. But in one selfless act, Auxiliary gave itself up, leaving Zellion with a Shardblade—a whispering echo of a once vibrant voice.

It's like having a book whose words have faded, yet the story lingers in the air, palpable and poignant. Zellion, now with a silent blade, carries the weight of Auxiliary's sacrifice. Each swing of the Shardblade is a tribute, each battle a memorial.

But let us not drown in sorrow, for the memory of Auxiliary lives on. In the silent moments, Zellion can almost hear the faintest whispers of wisdom, courage, and perhaps even a bit of well-meaning sarcasm, a remnant of a bond that even death couldn’t wholly sever.

Love

This is a tale of love and loss, of a warrior and a Spren whose story is etched not only in steel but in the very soul. Zellion, with each step, honors the memory of Auxiliary, a Spren who proved that true bravery often lies not in the heat of battle but in the sacrifices made for friends.

So here's to the silent heroes, to the Auxiliarys of the world, whose actions speak louder than any words could. In the quiet of Zellion's heart, the Spren’s legacy continues, a silent sentinel guiding a warrior on a path of honor and remembrance. May the winds of Roshar carry the tale of Auxiliary, a Spren as selfless as the highstorms are relentless. Cheers to you, dear Spren, for in your silence, you have spoken volumes.
