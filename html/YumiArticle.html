Yumi's New Nightmare

Painter was only the first of her challenges

Posted by Harmony's Lover on November 11th, 2023

Painter is one the best friends of Yumi. The drew close together living each others' lives. Painter has been jealous here and there as Yumi has met more men and made more friends.

He will always be our Nightmare Painter. Yumi can't let him go.

Lucky for us, they are pregnant! (no confirmations yet, but Yumi hasn't been seen publically except wearing baggy clothes)

The Art Thief

Knowing the lying past of Painter, we have to be careful what their son could get up to. Even with a great artist of a father, could the expectations turn their son to the life of crime? Stealing art and claiming it as his own!

We have to rely on Yumi's tenderheartedness. Several of Painter's friends would offer support if Painter comes off a little too mean. Don't fret! They will turn out alright.

Sunburns

Another consideration is the new sun. Painter has lived his whole life under the shadow of lost souls. Literally seeing no sunlight. How can this affect the experience of their kids?

Hopefully they invent sunscreen soon on Komashi or else we could see some more pain inflicted because of Painter, genetics or otherwise. May their love grow brighter!
